A Life Interrupted

At just 27 years old, Luigi Mangione should be building his future, pursuing his dreams, and contributing to society. Instead, he finds himself caught in a nightmare of indefinite detention, his constitutional rights seemingly forgotten by the very system designed to protect them.

Since December 9, 2024, Luigi has been denied the fundamental right to due process that forms the cornerstone of our justice system. No bail hearing. No trial date. No resolution in sight. Each passing day represents not just time lost, but a direct violation of the principles upon which our nation was founded.

Behind the statistics and legal terminology is a real person—a son, a friend, a community member—whose life hangs in limbo while bureaucratic wheels turn at an agonizingly slow pace. The emotional and psychological toll of indefinite detention cannot be overstated, both for Luigi and for those who care about him.
